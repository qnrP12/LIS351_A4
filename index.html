Cultural Experience

One of the most popular cultural experiences in Korea is visiting Gyeongbokgung Palace while wearing a traditional hanbok. Many visitors rent a hanbok and walk around the palace to take beautiful photos and experience Korean history more closely. If you wear a hanbok, you can also enter Gyeongbokgung Palace for free, which makes the experience even more special.

Recommended Destinations

Seongsu-dong

Seongsu-dong is one of Seoul's trendiest places, known for having various pop-up stores, stylish cafes, and popular restaurants. It's also a great place for shopping and taking photos.

Han River

The Han River is one of the best places to relax in Seoul. Many people enjoy having a picnic, riding bikes, or simply watching the sunset along the river. It is a perfect spot to spend time with friends or family.
